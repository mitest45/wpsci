Manage your hotel booking seamlessly!

== Description ==

With the WP Self Check-In Pro you manage your bookings, get guests data dynamically with the documents along with all members details.
For quick info you can see the settings tab of the plugin to get to know about shortcodes and the email tag. 
Firstly just create any page and add the shortcode to it then assign the page from the plugin setting tab.

= Features =

* Collect guests information with a form link.
* Create new bookings, that later can be modified.
* Receive identity documents from your guests.
* Documents can be asked or not, you get full control over it.
* Upload receipt for each booking and easily manage them.
* Compatible with MotoPress Hotel Booking plugin.
* Manage guests information for MotoPress Hotel Booking.
* Collect perfectly organized guests information legally formed as per Italian law.
* Notify your guest with customized emails.
* Collect guests data for quality assistance and export into a CSV file.
